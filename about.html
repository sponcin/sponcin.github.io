About Me

Hello, kind reader, and welcome to the portion of my programming portfolio where I would like to introduce myself. My name is Stephanie and am a native Rhode Islander and a 2016 URI alum. I am currently a Textile Designer and a volunteer for Ten Lives Cat Rescue which is a 501(c)(3) non profit cat rescue.

I love cats and currenty have two of my own, Daphne and Velma, who were both adopted from Ten Lives Cat Rescue in 2019 as kittens. Some of my hobbies include sewing, knitting and crocheting. I have a YouTube channel where I share knit and crochet patterns for free! Below are some images of my most recent creations, including a cat couch that will be auctioned off in April 2021 for Ten Lives. I also recently got back into reading in my free time and am currently reading The Invisible Life of Addie Larue.

I also have a B.A. in French and studied abroad in Paris at Sorbonne University for the summer of 2015. I was able to live with a host family and travel with others in the study abroad program to Normandie, the Loire Valley, London and Munich. It was an amazing experience and I can't wait to go back!
